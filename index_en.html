REPLAS MEXICO

We reprocess plastic materials, with the best technology in high quality equipment.

Mission:

The commitment of Replas México, is to produce recycled plastic materials through the pelletizing process fulfilling the requirements of our customers under the required regulations, through a quality management system in a process of continuous improvement.

Vision:

Become a leading company in the recycling of plastic nationally and internationally incorporating new transformation processes contributing to the care of the environment.



Resources we have:


Pelletizing machines with capacity of 450, 365 and 275 kg / hr.
